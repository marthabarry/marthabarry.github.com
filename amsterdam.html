ABOUT THE TRIP

In March 2013, I joined a group of students from my university (Massachusetts College of Art and Design) on a trip to the Netherlands. We went with two of our professors in the Graphic Design department on a "travel-course" dedicated to studying the graphic design culture in the Netherlands. We spent 8 days in Amsterdam, and over those 8 days, I collected images of things that I found beautiful or inspiring. Some of these images may seem insignificant but they hold meaning to me. Through this brief visual guide, I want to take you on a journey through this city through my eyes. These are the things that I notice as I travel, and these are the things that I choose to remember. The images were taken using a Canon 7D and my iPhone 4.
